Wistia Podcast Player

About

My last project at Wistia as product designer was to design a new audio player to support the company's shift into podcasting. Our goal was to ship a powerful, embeddeable audio player with rich podcasting features — all without sacrificing the simplicity and elegance of Wistia's much-admired video player. Challenges

Shortly after co-founding The Tourism Technology Co., a company dedicated to building lightweight and simple software for the tourism industry, my colleagues and I began work on Whereabouts, our flagship product. From its inception to its current iteration, I’ve served as Whereabouts’ Chief Product Officer, sole designer, researcher, and occasional people manager.
